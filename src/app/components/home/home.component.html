About Rapid Pharmaceuticals

Founded in 1997 and relaunched with a renewed vision, Rapid Pharmaceuticals is a trusted name in pharmaceutical distribution—now operating through a dynamic PCD (Propaganda Cum Distribution) franchise model across India.

We specialize in delivering high-quality, affordable medicines sourced from WHO-GMP and ISO-certified manufacturers. Our product portfolio spans major therapeutic segments, designed to meet the evolving needs of healthcare providers, pharmacies, and institutions nationwide.

With a focus on transparency, scalability, and partner success, Rapid empowers franchise associates with exclusive territory rights, marketing support, and a structured product catalog built for clarity and growth.

We’re not just building a network—we’re building a movement for smarter, faster, and more accountable healthcare delivery.
