About Rapid Pharmaceuticals

Founded in 1997 and relaunched with a renewed vision, Rapid Pharmaceuticals is a trusted name in pharmaceutical distribution—now operating through a dynamic PCD (Propaganda Cum Distribution) franchise model across India.

We specialize in delivering high-quality, affordable medicines sourced from WHO-GMP and ISO-certified manufacturers. Our product portfolio spans major therapeutic segments, designed to meet the evolving needs of healthcare providers, pharmacies, and institutions nationwide.

With a focus on transparency, scalability, and partner success, Rapid empowers franchise associates with exclusive territory rights, marketing support, and a structured product catalog built for clarity and growth. Whether you're an aspiring entrepreneur or an established distributor, Rapid offers the platform, products, and trust to help you thrive.

We’re not just building a network—we’re building a movement for smarter, faster, and more accountable healthcare delivery.

Why Choose Us

Rapid Pharmaceuticals is proud to operate through a robust PCD (Propaganda Cum Distribution) franchise model—empowering entrepreneurs, distributors, and medical representatives to build their own pharma businesses with the strength of our brand and product portfolio.

  • Established Legacy: With roots dating back to 1997, we bring decades of trust, experience, and credibility to every franchise partnership.
  • Certified Manufacturing: Our products are sourced from WHO-GMP and ISO-compliant manufacturers, ensuring consistent quality and regulatory confidence.
  • Wide Product Range: We offer a diverse catalog across major therapeutic segments—giving franchise partners the flexibility to target multiple markets.
  • Monopoly Rights: We provide exclusive territory rights to our franchise partners, helping them grow without internal competition.
  • Marketing Support: From promotional materials to strategic guidance, we equip our partners with the tools they need to succeed.
  • Low Investment, High Growth: Our PCD model is designed for scalability—allowing partners to start lean and expand rapidly.

Join Rapid Pharmaceuticals and become part of a growing network that delivers quality, trust, and opportunity—one franchise at a time.
