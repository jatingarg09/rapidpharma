Company Overview

Established in 1997, Rapid Pharmaceuticals is a trusted pharmaceutical distributor operating exclusively across India. While we do not manufacture our own products, we collaborate with certified manufacturers and deliver high-quality medicines under our own brand name.

Our mission is to ensure the timely and reliable supply of effective, safe, and affordable medicines to healthcare providers, distributors, and retailers throughout India — all under a brand you can trust.
