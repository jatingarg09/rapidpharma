Frequently Asked Questions

What is a PCD Pharma Franchise? add

A PCD Pharma Franchise allows distributors to sell pharma products with monopoly rights in their region. You get to build an independent business leveraging our established brand and high-quality product portfolio.

How can I apply for franchise rights? add

Simply fill out the inquiry form below. Our business development team will contact you promptly with details on available territories, product lists, and the onboarding process.

Do you provide promotional support? add

Yes, we provide comprehensive support including visual aids, MR bags, product catch covers, LBLs (Leave Behind Literatures), samples, digital creatives, and thorough product training.

Are your products DCGI-approved? add

Absolutely. All formulations are DCGI-approved and manufactured in highly regulated, WHO-GMP compliant facilities to ensure the highest standards of safety and efficacy.

Do you provide a formal monopoly agreement? add

Yes. Once territory availability is confirmed and terms are finalized, we issue a signed, legally binding monopoly agreement to secure exclusive distribution and marketing rights for your specified district.

What are the payment and billing terms? add

All PCD franchise orders operate on a standard B2B cash-and-carry or advance-payment basis. Wholesale billing starts immediately upon confirmation of the order sheet and payment receipt.

Who handles transportation and freight? add

Orders are shipped Ex-works from our central hub. Transportation and freight charges are borne by the distributor (freight-to-pay basis) using reliable logistics partners.
